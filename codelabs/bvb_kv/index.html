  • Elephant Alarm

This project is very helpful for the people who are staying in coorg, often elephants are camping at farms and coffee estates in Kodagu,most of the farmers have stopped farming activities after being unable to bear the losses incurred by the menace.The farmers have lost coffee, banana plantations etc., following the elephant menace, in many cases they will kill the farmers and workers also. So there is a demand for permanent solution to check the menace.

From "Elephant security" project we can get the alert before elephants enter the farm and we can avoid the loss and pain. We are going to install ultrasonic sensors across the boundaries(fence) of the farm, so it can detect the movement with a specified distance,and we are using a buzzer(we can use RGB with a buzzer) to get alert.

If an elephant passes near the fence the ultrasonic sensor will detect and buzzer will alert us so that they can take the necessary actions to avoid the menace.
