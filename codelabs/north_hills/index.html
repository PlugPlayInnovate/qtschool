Objective:

North Hills International School (NHIS), believe that all children have unique talents, be it in academics, sports, communication, technology, art, craft or drama - every child is special. Our mission is to recognize and enhance every individual's special talent. Our teaching methodology ensures that students are exposed to a lively and dynamic learning environment, thus giving them the prospect to discover and realize their potential and to accomplish success.

Vision:

"Children are our future... We are dedicated to their Success"

Mission:

  • To build the child's future in this ever-changing world.
  • To create a safe and nurturing environment of mutual respect.
  • To inspire students to achieve academic excellence and personal growth.
  • To facilitate student-centred learning.
  • To work in collaboration with home and community.
