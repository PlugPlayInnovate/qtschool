About Us : North Hills International School (NHIS), believe that all children have unique talents, be it in academics, sports, communication, technology, art, craft or drama - every child is special. Our mission is to recognize and enhance every individual's special talent. Our teaching methodology ensures that students are exposed to a lively and dynamic learning environment, thus giving them the prospect to discover and realize their potential and to accomplish success.

Vision: "Children are our future... We are dedicated to their Success"

Mission:

  • To build the child's future in this ever-changing world.
  • To create a safe and nurturing environment of mutual respect.
  • To inspire students to achieve academic excellence and personal growth.
  • To facilitate student-centred learning.
  • To work in collaboration with home and community.

  • Bangalore Tech Summit 2019

The 22nd edition of Bengaluru Tech Summit was held from 18th Nov -20th Nov 2019 which witnessed a wide range of technology. Students actively participated in the workshop held on 18th Nov 2019. Students designed and built Soccer Bot and BasketBall Counter projects using the MIT App inventor ( Code2Play ) and presented the same excellently.

  • Orientation Program

Team QTPI were invited for The Parents Orientation Program held at North Hills International School, Bengaluru.   • Python with QtPi

Team qtpi conducted a python session in NHIS, where the students of grade 8,9 and 10 were taught about the fundamentals of programming, how programming becomes easier with python. Students had hands-on experience on python by solving the task with utmost enthusiasm. The entire session was interactive which showcased the eagerness of the students to learn new things.
