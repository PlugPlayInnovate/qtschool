About us : Bhashyam Blooms is a school that thinks and acts differently. Bhashyam Blooms firmly believes quality education leads to a healthy society. It understands and respects every child's individuality and strives hard to ensure that their individuality is sustained. The focus is always on creating a synergy of individual ideas into a single powerful and productive ideology that is called a society. Every child is talented and is always brimming with ideas. At Bhashyam these ideas are received and given due consideration. Blooms offers the students an exclusive learning experience with global standards.

Vision: "We strongly believe that intelligence plus character is the goal of true education"

Mission: "Our mission is to not only inculcate basic moral values and courtesies but also nourish the inherent talent of the students"

Moto: "Our school's motto is "Take on the world!!" As we always encourage our students to dream big, we wish to instil the quality of aiming high in our children's minds."
