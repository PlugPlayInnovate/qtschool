About us : QtPi was established in 2016 and since then it has collaborated with 40+ schools, providing training in Robotics and STEAM. QtPi has achieved significant results across the schools it is collaborating with by providing not just theoretical knowledge but by providing practical and hands-on experience with the kit provided by it. In 2018 QtPi has stepped in Andhra Pradesh by collaborating with one of the prestigious schools, "Bhashyam Blooms"

  • Vision: "We strongly believe that intelligence plus character is the goal of true education"
  • Mission: "Our mission is to not only inculcate basic moral values and courtesies but also nourish the inherent talent of the students"
  • Moto: "Our school's motto is "Take on the world!!" As we always encourage our students to dream big, we wish to instil the quality of aiming high in our children's minds."
