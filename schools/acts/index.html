Objective:

The institution strives to impart all-round and sound education leading to spiritual, intellectual, physical and cultural development of the child.The school curriculum provides opportunities for learning while cherishing the uniqueness and individuality of each child. The school environment fosters creative thinking, decision making, compassion, caring and respect for ourselves, other people and property.

Mission:

ACTS Secondary School is committed to training children of all socio-economic levels through quality education that will transform the whole person and equip them to face the challenges of today's varied contexts. ACTS Secondary School is a member of the ACTS group of Institutions, and is committed to "integrated holistic education." We believe that – "Education should go beyond mere transfer of knowledge and skills to transforming people, and building responsible citizens of tomorrow. With Christian values as its foundation, ACTS offers its service to people of all communities regardless of caste or creed".
