QtPi was established in 2016 and since then it has collaborated with 40+ schools, providing training in Robotics and STEAM. QtPi has achieved significant results across the schools it is collaborating with by providing not just theoretical knowledge but by providing practical and hands-on experience with the kit provided by it. In 2019 QtPi has stepped in Karnataka by collaborating with one of the prestigious schools, Sri Chaitanya School.
