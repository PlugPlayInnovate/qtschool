About us : We, at Surya The Global School, believe that learning changes lives and it's imperative that we provide a head start to the young lives in our school through transformative educational programs. Our programs recognize that no two children are the same and thus provide the space, encouragement, and the learning environment that allow the students to learn, grow, and develop at their comfortable pace.

We encourage positive relationships and mutual respects among & between students and staff. Our teachers are trained to provide a supportive and challenging environment for the students to make the right choices and appropriate decisions.

  • Vision: "To emerge as a pioneering educational institution that blends academic excellence and universal human values with a global outlook"
  • Mission: "To provide a nurturing environment for the holistic growth and development of every facet of the students. Surya The Global School endows their minds with ethics and guides them to evolve into socially conscious global citizens of tomorrow."
  • Moto: "Our school's motto is "Take on the world!!" As we always encourage our students to dream big, we wish to instil the quality of aiming high in our children's minds."
